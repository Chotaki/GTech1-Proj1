Gameplay

Easy to play

osu! is a very easy game and easy to understand, it requires :
-only 1 or 2 finger(s) on the keyboard
-a mouse/graphic tablet

Difficulty

The difficulty can vary from 1 to 10+ stars,
the gameplay aside shows how hard it can get

Map editor

Rules

Basic rules

Click the circles in the correct order and at the perfect timing to earn as much points as possible!
Stay cool and do not get overwhelmed by the speed of the game!

Stats & Combo

The goal is to earn as much points as possible and to do so, you'll have to grind a combo. A combo increases the amount of point you'll earn by clicking the circles.
A little advice, accuracy will save your combo!

Sliders

You'll encounter sliders, keep your cursor inside the moving circle that follows along the slider!
You have to get used to the perfect speed my boi.

Spins

Spins are insane treasure points, the ultimate dream of any kind of Pirate!
Spin as fast as you can to score as many points as possible!

Difficulty? Yes Sir!

Finally, osu! is an insanely difficult game, there are incredibly difficult levels but still, a few amazing players can complete them!
It's your turn to join the Game!
